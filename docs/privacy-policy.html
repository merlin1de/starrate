Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

StarRate ("the App") is developed by Mathias Mischler ("we", "us"). This policy describes how data is handled when you use the StarRate Android app.

1. Data You Provide

To use StarRate you connect it to your own Nextcloud server. The App stores your server URL, login name, and an app-specific password locally on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256). These credentials are never sent to us or any third party.

2. Photo Data

All photos, ratings, and color labels are stored on your Nextcloud server. The App displays them via a WebView and writes metadata (star ratings, color labels) back to your server. No photo data leaves your Nextcloud instance.

3. Guest / Share Links

When you open a guest share link, the App connects directly to the Nextcloud server that created the link. No account or personal data is required.

4. Advertising (Free Version Only)

The free version of StarRate displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android settings under Google > Ads. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

The paid version ("StarRate Pro") does not include any advertising or ad-related SDKs.

5. Analytics & Tracking

StarRate does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs beyond what is described in section 4.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data remains on your device and your Nextcloud server. We do not operate any backend servers and have no access to your data. Credentials are encrypted at rest using Android Keystore-backed encryption.

7. Children's Privacy

StarRate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at this URL.
